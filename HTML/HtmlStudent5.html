Biography and Background

Dehan Kriel was born in Johannesburg, and attended school in Durban. He is studying BIT(IS) with the informatics electives because he is passionate about cybersecurity and wants to help protect businesses from digital threats.

Hobbies

He enjoys spending his free time playing video games, especially strategy and role-playing games that challenge his problem-solving skills. When he's not gaming, he likes hiking and exploring nature, always looking for new trails to conquer. He also has a passion for photography, capturing scenic landscapes and city life through his lens.
