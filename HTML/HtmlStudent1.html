Biography and Background

I am Mia Hand and I was born in Pretoria. I went to school in Doringkloof. I study BIS(IS) with the electives of Informatics, because I enjoy coding more than I enjoy studying pure theory.

Hobbies

I enjoy expressing my creativity through drawing and writing stories, bringing my ideas to life in different ways. Playing video games is another favorite pastime, offering both entertainment and a way to explore new worlds. I also love going for walks, taking in the fresh air and clearing my mind. Collecting keychains is a small but meaningful hobby for me, as each one holds a memory or a special connection.
