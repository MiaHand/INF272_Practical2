Biography and Background

Samantha van Es was born in Port Elizabeth, and attended school in Bloemfontein. She is studying BIT(IS) with the informatics electives because she has a strong interest in data analysis and wants to help organizations make smarter, data-driven decisions.

Hobbies

She is a big fan of video games, particularly adventure and open-world RPGs where she can immerse herself in different stories. She also loves reading fantasy novels, often finding inspiration in the worlds and characters she encounters. In her downtime, she enjoys baking and trying out new dessert recipes, sharing her sweet creations with friends and family.
